Experience, grow, and create friendship. One trip at a time.

  • Choose an Activity

    Choose where you want to do. What new skills do you want to learn? What activity would you like to refine your skills in? What kind of risk are you willing to accept? What environment do you want to partake in?

  • Pick a Location

    Choose where you want to go. Identify the pros and cons of traveling to each location and the experience the location will help create. Consider the costs of each location as well as the convenience.

  • Identify a Group

    Is this a personal trip? An open club trip? Publicize the trip and gauge interest, then identify who will be enjoying the experience you are creating.

  • Find Funding

    With an amazing activity and location in mind, how are you going to fund the adventure? If it is an official club trip, then work with your club to put in the club's term budget. If it is a personal trip consider appling for the Schlitz, Vale, and/or Leadership funds.
